SimpleSynth

Download

SimpleSynth is a small and fast synth, perfect for when you want to try out a musical idea quickly. It's got a complete general MIDI instrument set built in, and it can load Soundfonts and DLS files.

Be warned though: I haven't updated it in a long time, so your mileage may vary under Lion and later. If you'd like to contribute fixes, you can find the source code on GitHub.

Released
5 September 2009

Requirements
